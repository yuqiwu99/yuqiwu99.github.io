Why Mathematical Modelling?

Throughout history, mathematics has been playing a giant role in sciences and business. Mathematical models are behind every single engineering and business problems. Yet traditionally, this is done by professionals and seems to be far away from most people. In order to encourage more and more people to utilize their mathematical talent to the world, establishing a mathematical modelling club is essential.
